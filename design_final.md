# SuperSolid Agency Overview

## The Quantum State of Creativity
Supersolid is a Sydney-based creative agency (founded in 2023) that positions itself at the intersection of fluid imagination and rigid logic—inspired by the **supersolid** quantum state of matter. They move beyond the traditional "creative vs. corporate" divide with their **Supervalid Thinking** framework.

### Core Philosophy: ROIdeas® (Return On Ideas)
A trademarked approach that treats creativity as a measurable business investment. Supersolid focuses on **calculated category disruption** to deliver financial results, such as securing startup funding or achieving massive social media reach.
